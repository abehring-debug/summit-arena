Prairie Peak College Fair

August 26 @ 10:00 am - 5:00 pm

Free – $10.00
  • « Ohio State High School Girls Basketball Final
  • Prairie Peak Farmers & Artisans Market »

Description: Explore your future at the Prairie Peak College Fair, where students and families can connect with representatives from colleges and universities nationwide. Gain insights into programs, admissions, scholarships, and campus life all in one place. This event is your gateway to discovering the perfect fit for your academic and career aspirations.

Date: March 15th, 2025 | 10 a.m. – 5 p.m.

Ticket Price: 

  • General Admission: Free
  • Optional Workshop Access: $10 (per session, includes specialized topics like financial aid or essay writing)

Features: 

  1. College Booths: Engage with representatives from local, regional, and national colleges and universities offering information about academic programs and admissions.
  2. Workshops and Seminars: Attend sessions on topics like financial aid, crafting the perfect college application, and choosing a major.
  3. On-the-Spot Admissions: Some colleges may offer instant decision opportunities—bring your transcripts and test scores!
  4. Scholarship Resources: Discover local and national scholarship opportunities with guidance on applications.
  5. Student Panels: Hear firsthand experiences from current students about life on various campuses.
  6. Networking Opportunities: Meet alumni and professionals to learn about career paths tied to specific majors.
  7. Virtual College Tours: Experience campuses through virtual reality or guided video presentations.
  8. Q&A Sessions: Ask admissions counselors about deadlines, test requirements, and tips for standing out.
  9. Parent Resources: Special sessions designed to help parents support their student’s transition to college.

Giveaways: Participate in raffles for college gear, books, or other prizes.
